# Vision: Efficient Workflows

A lot of current AI usage assumes that bigger context and bigger prompts are the only way to solve complex tasks. 

`claude-mileage` is a project focused on **workflow efficiency**. 

We believe that for the vast majority of software engineering and knowledge work, the constraint isn't the model's intelligence—it's the user's **context management**. When you hit a rate limit, it is often because of redundant context, repeated logs, or unnecessary file content.

### Our North Star
1. **Constraint as a Practice:** Constraints encourage clarity. By managing mileage, we focus on writing better prompts and defining smaller, clearer tasks.
2. **Local Sovereignty:** Your project's core information belongs on your disk, not buried in an ever-growing cloud thread history that adds cost with every message.
3. **Terminal Native:** A tool for people who live in the CLI. Fast, local-first, and composable.
4. **Local Preparation:** Pre-processing context locally before calling the official CLI binary.

### The "Mileage" Metric
We measure success by "Utility per Token." If we can help a user complete a complex refactor by being more surgical, we have succeeded. 

We want to help you work more efficiently within your current workflow. By reducing waste, we make high-quality reasoning more effective.

> **Internal Shorthand:** "Mileage over Max" is our way of describing this efficiency-first workflow design. It simply means we prioritize reducing waste over encouraging bigger context.

# Vision: Efficient Workflows

`token-tengu` (token-tengu 👺) is a pivot toward **workflow efficiency**. 

A lot of current AI usage assumes that bigger context and bigger prompts are the only way to solve complex tasks. We believe that for most software engineering and knowledge work, the real bottleneck is **context management**. When a user hits a limit, it is often due to redundant context, repeated data, or sloppy task boundaries.

### Our North Star
1. **Constraint as a Practice:** Constraints encourage clarity. By managing mileage, we focus on writing better prompts and defining smaller, clearer tasks.
2. **Local Sovereignty:** Your project's core information belongs on your disk, not buried in an ever-growing cloud thread history.
3. **Terminal Native:** Built for people who live in the CLI. Fast, local-first, and composable.
4. **The Token-Tengu Spirit:** A subtle, disciplined guardian for your workflow. Inspectable, sharp, and focused on the task at hand.

### Success Metric: Utility per Token
We measure success by "Utility per Token." If we can help a user complete a complex refactor in fewer messages by being more surgical, the project has succeeded. We want to help you work more efficiently within the workflow you already use.

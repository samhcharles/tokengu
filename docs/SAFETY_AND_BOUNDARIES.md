# Safety and Boundaries

`token-tengu` is built on a foundation of respect for the underlying service (Anthropic's Claude).

### 1. Respecting TOS
We do not automate the "clicking" of buttons or the scraping of web interfaces. We wrap the official CLI, which is the intended way for terminal users to interact with the service.

### 2. No Hidden Paywalls
This project is open source and will always be free. We do not sit in the middle of your transactions. Your relationship is with Anthropic; our relationship is with your local workflow.

### 3. Data Privacy
Because this is a local wrapper, your data stays on your machine. We do not "phone home," we do not collect your prompts, and we do not see your API keys or session tokens.

### 4. Intentionality
By forcing a "Context Preview," we encourage users to think about what they are sending. This reduces "junk" data being sent to Anthropic's servers and ensures your usage is high-signal.
